Our Mission

Viva con Agua Uganda is dedicated to increasing sustainable access to Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) services for Ugandans in schools and communities through joyful experiences.

Our Unique Approach

We use Universal Languages for Behavioral Change (UL4BC) to make learning about WASH fun and memorable:

Football for WASH

Combining the power of football with WASH education

Art for WASH

Using visual arts to convey WASH messages

Dance for WASH

Engaging communities through dance and movement

WASH Heroes

Comic books featuring relatable characters promoting WASH practices
